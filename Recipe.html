Simple Recipes - Fry an Egg

The Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Seasoning (e.g. Salt and Pepper)

The Procedure

  1. Preheat 9" frying pan at medium heat
  2. Lightly butter the frying pan
  3. Crack egg on rounded corner and pour egg gently in pan (repeat for desired number of eggs
  4. Season eggs as desired
  5. When egg is semi-firm (~1 minute), flip the egg
  6. After ~30 seconds, flip the egg(s) onto a plate and serve hot
