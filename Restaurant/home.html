Cove Cafe is housed in one of Mountain View's original beach cottages. Our seaside location offers a spectacular view of the Pacific ocean where the waves are smooth and gentle. We serve breakfast thru dinner with seasonal menu changes by Chef Doug Hecker. Come to "Our Cottage"

Our cottage is the perfect place for mid-morning breakfast with a group of friends, a large group for lunch to celebrate a co-worker, or a romantic dinner for 2 in one of our hidden alcoves (each has their own view of the ocean).
